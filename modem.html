Modem was formed in 2012 by 11 students across different programmes. Their vision was to deliver entertaining and informative radio for the students at ITU, a vision we have upheld and expanded upon since then. Likewise, our studio has expanded from a tiny room on the second floor to anywhere in the building as we have gone mobile. This increased mobility allows us to get closer to our listeners right at the heart of ITU.

We are always looking for people that share our passion for making radio while having fun. If you want to contribute, please get in touch through any of our social media platforms or write an email to our main address radiomodem.dk@gmail.com or any of the board members.
